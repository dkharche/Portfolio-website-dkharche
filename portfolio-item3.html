Autonomous self-driving car

This project is a virtual self-driving car project using the Pygame Python Module. The goal is to create a self-driving car that can navigate a virtual environment in the Pygame environment. The car will be able to detect obstacles in the environment, such as walls, and then make decisions on how to best avoid them.

The car will also be able to detect other cars in the environment, and determine the best way to navigate around them, as well as detect traffic lights and signs, and respond accordingly. Additionally, the car will be able to recognize and respond to user input, allowing the user to control the car's direction and speed. Finally, the car will be able to learn from its experiences, improving its ability to navigate the environment.
